Our Product:

We are creating a photo based social media application which, unlike many other social media apps, is to be a safe, educational environment where users can share images of animals an objects. Aimed at all age groups, we want SocialIO to be a place for users to have fun, show off their photography skills and their item collections, as well as being an educational platform for our younger users.

The Application:

Timeline

This is where users will view most of thier content and using the upvote, downvote and comment functions, can interact with other users posts. At the bottom of the screen is the navbar which is how users will change between SocialIO's different pages.

Profile

Profile pages allow users to view all of the content from a particular user, it also displays the rank of the user, any rewards they have obtained and the profile's statistics such as followers and following. The arrangement of the content can be changed using the two buttons above the posts to give either a timeline or grid view.

Search

The search page is how users will find new profiles and explore content from users that they aren't following. This page will display posts from the top users in categories similar to those posted to by the user and the search bar allows for users to search for specific content and or profiles.

Camera

The built in camera function allows users to take stunning photos on the go, and also upload images from their existing library

What Makes Us Different?

Safety

Our application is a safe and ethical photosharing enivronment where users of all age can feel safe and secure sharing their photos with their friends and the wider community. SocialIO has strict content restrictions on to protect our users from potential trolling and cyber-bullying. This allows younger audiences to be able to use and enjoy SocialIO to it's fullest, engaging in both the social, and education aspects of the app.

Ethics

SocialIO doesn't allow users to upload pictures of people so you never have to worry about an embarrassing photo being posted to our platform. We will use sophisticated image recognition software to detect and remove posts containing people, and failing that, we have a team of admins who will moderate content reported as innapropriate by users using the in-app reporting functionality.

Gameplay

We wanted to make SocialIO appealing and fun to use for all age groups, so we implemented a scoring system to rank user's posts within the category of image. Keep posting images to improve your score and improve your rank to win in-app rewards! These rewards will be cosmetic changes to the interface of the app and other cool and unique in-app content.
